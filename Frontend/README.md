# Dawai 💊

**Dawai** is a premium centralized digital platform hub designed to improve healthcare accessibility in the Gaza Strip. It bridges the gap between citizens, certified pharmacies, and the Pharmacists Syndicate through a unified, transparent, and reliable digital ecosystem.

## 🌟 Vision & Purpose

In the face of challenging conditions, Dawai leverages technology to save time, effort, and lives. The platform aims to eliminate the struggle of finding essential medicines by providing real-time data on availability, verified pricing, and pharmacy locations.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🔍 Advanced Medicine Search

- **Bilingual Search:** Find medicines using both Arabic and English names.
- **Real-time Availability:** Instantly see which pharmacies have the required medication in stock.
- **Verified Pricing:** Access official prices approved by the Pharmacists Syndicate to ensure transparency.

### 🏥 Certified Pharmacy Directory

- **Interactive Directory:** Explore a comprehensive list of certified pharmacies across Gaza.
- **Operating Hours & On-Call Info:** Check real-time status of pharmacies.
- **Direct Navigation:** One-click integration with Google Maps for easy location tracking.

### 📢 Digital Health Center

- **Official Bulletins:** Stay updated with authenticated news, and health alerts from the Syndicate.
- **Exclusive Pharmacy Offers:** A dedicated space for pharmacies to showcase discounts and humanitarian health services.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Frontend Core:** [React.js](https://reactjs.org/)
- **UI Framework:** [Material UI (MUI)](https://mui.com/) - Providing a premium, modern design language.
- **Animations:** [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) - For smooth transitions and interactive elements.
- **Navigation:** [React Router DOM](https://reactrouter.com/)

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The project follows a **Premium Indigo & Teal** aesthetic, focusing on:

- **Responsiveness:** Seamless experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
- **Accessibility:** Intuitive RTL (Right-to-Left) and LTR (Left-to-Right) support.
- **Aesthetics:** Clean card-based layouts, glassmorphism effects, and professional typography (Inter & Cairo).
